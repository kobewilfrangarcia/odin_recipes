Joke Lasagna

Garfield's favorite dish.

So, been reading those comics again? Before you apologize to Jon about your cravings, let me share you a secret family recipe of the iconic pasta dish.

Before we start, I must tell you a 5 paragraph introduction of how the recipe came to be before I provide any instruction

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 28 ounce tomato paste
  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 egg
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 pound ground Beef Lean

Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water.
  3. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, and Italian seasoning.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil and cook lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. Assemble the ingredients.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes.
